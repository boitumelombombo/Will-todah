About Will Todah

Will Todah is an upcoming South African musician. Growing up in a christian family, Will Todah (real name Boitumelo Willie Mbombo) got introduced to playing keyboard and singing in church. Todah moved from the churchy sounds and got introduced to jazz music when he went to varsity. His love for contemporary gospel, Neosoul and chill hop was then sparked and has a very strong influence in his sound. Will Todah is a versatile musician still trying to figure out a way to convey the message of love, peace and harmony to the world through this universal umbilical cord that is MUSIC. His latest and first single called "Calm through the storm", was released in September 2019.
